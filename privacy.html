Privacy Policy

This page is used to inform visitors regarding the policies with the collection and use of information if anyone decides to use Guitar Fretboard: Scales app.

If you choose to use the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The information collected is used for improving the app.

What Information Does the App Collect?

The app does not collect or use any personal information. The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect analytics data and Sentry for error and crash reporting.

Please refer to Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy and Sentry Privacy Policy for more details.

Crash and error reports are used to improve the app. In addition to error information, Sentry collects device metadata such as manufacturer, OS version, etc., which is used to investigate the root cause of crashes or errors.

Analytics events collect information about the use of app features such as navigation to a page in the app, toggling switches to enable/disable features, app session duration, and the number of sessions per user per day. All of these analytics events do not collect personally identifiable information. Collected data is used to improve the app by identifying which features are most used and which are not used at all for example.

All settings and user added data in the app are stored locally on the device and can be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data.

Advertising

The app uses AdMob to serve advertisements. Please refer to AdMob's Privacy Policies for more information.
