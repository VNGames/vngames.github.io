Guitar fretboard: scales

Guitar Fretboard app is designed to help you learn scales. Allows to customize every aspect of displaying a scale or chord notes/intervals on a guitar fretboard. Also shows each scale degree chords and modes with ability to highlight a chord on a scale. Important: doesn't show how to play chords, shows only intervals of a chord and it is up to you to choose fingerings. Great for learning chords in details. Not great as a simple reference. Application is catered more to learning scales than chords unless you want to see all the chord notes on a fretboard. Plays notes when they are tapped on fretboard.

Features:
- 45+ scales (Major scale modes, Harmonic minor scale modes, Melodic minor scale modes, Major/Minor pentatonic, Exotic scales, Bebop scales) and 35+ chords
- Ability to add custom scales and chords
- 35+ tunings
- Ability to add custom tunings
- Allows to change scale palette and add custom scale palettes
- Allows to change fretboard style and add customs styles
- 4 view modes including (no text, notes, intervals, both notes and intervals)
- Allows to highlight intervals of a scale
- Show/Hide notes outside of scale
- Left handed mode
- Zoom (use pinch gesture to zoom fretboard)
- Play notes (guitar and bass)
- Focus on scale notes only on specific area of a fretboard
- Dark theme
